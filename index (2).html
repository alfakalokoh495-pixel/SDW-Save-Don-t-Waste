How to Save Don't Waste

Simple, science-backed actions that actually move the needle. Click each card to see your impact.

Water

The average American uses 80–100 gallons per day. Here’s how to cut it in half.

  • 5-minute showers = 25 gallons saved
  • Fix drips = 3,000 gallons/year
  • Full loads only = 20% less water

Energy

U.S. homes waste 30% of electricity. Quick wins add up fast.

  • LED bulbs = 75% less energy
  • Unplug vampires = $150/year saved
  • Smart thermostat = 10% bill cut

Waste & Plastic

Americans throw away 4.5 lbs of trash daily. You can change that.

  • Reusable bags = 1,000 plastic bags/year
  • Compost = 30% of landfill diverted
  • Buy in bulk = 50% less packaging
